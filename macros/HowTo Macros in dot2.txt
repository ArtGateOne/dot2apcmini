### Macros

Copy this Macros to your dot2 Macro Folder.  
In Windows:  
`C:\ProgramData\MA Lighting Technologies\dot2\dot2_V_1.9\macros`

in dot2 Console enter Import-Command for each Macro. It is simular to GrandMA 2.  
Have a Look at:  
https://help2.malighting.com/Page/grandMA2/ei_import_commandline/en/3.3 
Example:  
`Import "macro_51_apcmini_palete_page5" At Macro 51`

in dot2 Console enter Export-Command for each Macro. It is simular to GrandMA 2.  
Have a Look at:  
https://help2.malighting.com/Page/grandMA2/ei_export_commandline/en/3.3
